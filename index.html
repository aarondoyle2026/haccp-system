🮒 Thawing Check Sheet

Record all frozen products being thawed. Core temp target 0–5°C after thawing.

*Frozen food must be defrosted in a refrigerated area <5°C.
*Core temperature must be checked with a thermometer and recorded — target 0°C→5°C.
*Thawed food to be heat treated must be processed within 24 hours of completion of thawing.
**Fresh Cream Cakes do not need to be probed after thawing once thawing guidelines have been followed (RQA 1034 Issue D dated 01:08)
